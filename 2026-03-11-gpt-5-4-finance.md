# OpenAI's GPT-5.4 Takes Finance by Storm

The numbers are staggering. OpenAI just dropped GPT-5.4 Thinking — and it just demolished the financial analysis benchmarks that matter most to the people who actually move money.

On OpenAI's internal investment banking benchmark (three-statement modeling), GPT-5.4 jumped from **43.7% to 87.3%**. That's not a marginal improvement. That's a paradigm shift in what AI can do with financial modeling.

## This Isn't a Chatbot. It's a Financial Analyst.

Let's be clear about what changed. Previous models could draft a model — maybe. GPT-5.4 Thinking builds three-statement models (income statement, balance sheet, cash flow), stress-tests assumptions, and outputs analysis that investment professionals can actually use. The 87.3% benchmark score represents tasks completed at professional analyst level with minimal human correction.

That's the story. The benchmark numbers tell you everything.

## ChatGPT for Excel: The Killer Feature

The feature that's going to make this inescapable for finance teams: **ChatGPT for Excel beta**.

It's embedded directly in spreadsheets. You select cells, ask questions in plain English, and it builds or updates models, runs scenario analysis, and generates outputs based on your formulas. No switching between tabs. No copying prompts. It's right there in the grid where your models live.

The Google Sheets version is coming soon — which means this is going mainstream fast.
